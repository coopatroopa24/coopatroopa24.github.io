SSEP Research Paper

Are Animals Better off in the Wild or Captivity?

There’s been a burning question that has been looming in the atmosphere that has been prominent now more than ever. Are animals better off in captivity or in the wild? What makes this question relevant is because of the recent events that have occurred at the Cincinnati Zoo with the gorilla and child. After the incident, many questioned if it was still safe for animals to stay in captive areas, such as zoos. Even before the incident, the debate on whether animals belong in the wild or not were basically half and half. There are indeed species that are endangered that may need human intervention to save their species. On the other hand, some animals are neglected and mistreated, and they are in need of being set “free” into the wild.

The Everyday Life of a Physical Therapist

Physical therapists are people who aid injured patients in enhancing movement and limiting pain. Rehabilitation and treatment of ill patients are two essential parts of being a physical therapist. Most of them actually work in offices, clinics, nursing homes, hospitals. The average physical therapist will spend most of their time on their feet and actively helping patients. To become a physical therapist, one would need a to have a Doctor of Physical Therapy degree and become licensed in their respective state. The job field for becoming a physical therapist is better than ever because the growth rate is at a staggering 36%.
